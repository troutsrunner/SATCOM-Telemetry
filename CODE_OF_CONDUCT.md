# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming community, we pledge to make participation in this project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or exclusionary comments
- Violent or threatening language
- Personal attacks or insults
- Public or private harassment

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that are not aligned with this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies both within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.
